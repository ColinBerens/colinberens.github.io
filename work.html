My 3D projects

Fluffy Nature

Fluffy nature is a showcase of my attempt to make a stylised scene in Unity. I used HDRP Shader graphs for the grass, leaves and water, and VFX Graphs for the blowing leaves. For texturing the windmill and the terrain I used Substance Painter. Modeling wise I used Blender and Tree It for the trees.
If you wanna see the timelapse of the process click the button below.

Artisan's Desk

The Artisan's desk is a showcase of my attempt in making handpainted textures that are not realistic. I used Blender for the model and Substance Painter for the textures.

Moustache Kart

The MoustacheKart is made using PBR textures. Modeling and Animation were done in Blender. And Texturing was done in Substance Painter.

Small Farm Diorama

That is my second Diorama. I put alot of effort in trying to make a story to this scene. It's not a bland showcase of things put into a scene. Alot of stuff tells a story. I think that is most important.
