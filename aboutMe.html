About Me

Hello! I’m Colin Berens, a creative designer and passionate game developer.
I'm currently studying independent game production at the Digital Arts and Entertainment College in Kortrijk, which has shaped my expertise and fueled my passion for game development.

Interests

Some movies have a distinctive style that can really help in games. I really love technology. Seeing where AI can help a developer in the future is really interesting.
